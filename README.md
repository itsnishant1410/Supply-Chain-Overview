# Supply-Chain-Overview
1. Headline
Interactive Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard for Revenue, Inventory, Sales, and Profit Performance Monitoring

2. Short Description
This Power BI dashboard provides a centralized view of supply chain performance by integrating key operational and financial metrics into a single interactive report. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor revenue generation, product sales trends, inventory availability, and profit margins while identifying areas for operational improvement.

Through dynamic visualizations, KPI cards, and drill-down analysis, users can evaluate business performance across products and inventory categories, track stock movement, and assess the impact of sales performance on overall profitability. The solution is designed to support data-driven decision-making, improve supply chain visibility, and enhance business efficiency through real-time analytical insights.

3.Tech Stack
Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard development
Power Query – Data transformation and cleansing
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI calculations and business metrics
Data Modeling – Relationship management and performance optimization
Supply Chain Analytics – Inventory, sales, revenue, and profitability analysis

Features
Interactive supply chain performance dashboard
Revenue, sales, and profit tracking across products
Inventory monitoring and stock-level analysis
KPI cards for quick performance evaluation
Dynamic filtering and slicers for customized analysis
Product-wise sales and profitability breakdown
Trend analysis for revenue and sales performance
Drill-down capabilities for detailed insights
Automated calculations using DAX measures
User-friendly and executive-focused dashboard design
